Minecraft is a popular 3D sandbox, block-based, "virtual Lego" game developed by Mojang Studios where players explore, craft, and build in an open world. With no fixed goals, it features survival, creative, adventure, and spectator modes. Players can mine resources, build, fight, and play with others.

In Survival mode, players must manage their health and hunger while defending themselves from hostile mobs like zombies and skeletons. This adds challenge and requires strategy to stay alive.

The game includes a detailed crafting system where collected resources are used to create tools, weapons, armor, and building materials. Crafting allows players to progress and build stronger structures.

Players can also explore special dimensions like the Nether and the End, which add adventure and new challenges to the gameplay.

With multiplayer servers, custom maps, and community-created mods, Minecraft offers endless creativity and interactive experiences for players of all ages.
